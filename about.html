Meet Daisy

Hi beautiful! My name is Daisy, and I'm the founder of DaisyMo Collective! I am a Photographer and the owner of DaisyMo Collective Studio.

From capturing moments behind the lens to orchestrating full-scale creative productions, my journey has been one of transformation and vision. I began my career as a photographer, honing a sharp eye for detail, storytelling, and composition. Over time, that same creative discipline led me into project management, where I learned to lead teams, manage budgets, and deliver high-impact results across timelines.

Whether it's building brand content, directing shoots, or managing cross-functional teams, I thrive where creativity and clarity meet. Welcome to my world — where every project is a canvas, and every result is intentional.

Today, I bring both worlds together as the founder and lead at my own studio — a space where creativity meets structure. My background in photography gives me a deep understanding of visual language, while my experience in project management ensures that every shoot, campaign, or client brief is executed with precision and purpose.

Our studio isn't just a rental space – it's a community where creativity flourishes and dreams become reality. Every detail has been carefully curated to inspire and support your artistic journey.
