FAQ

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially for weekends and popular time slots. However, we often have same-day availability for weekday bookings.

What's included in the studio rental?

Basic studio rental includes access to our space, basic lighting setup, props, and backdrops. Professional lighting equipment and makeup services are available as add-ons.

Do you offer makeup services?

Yes! We have professional makeup artists available by appointment. This can be added to any studio rental package or booked as a standalone service.

Can I bring my own equipment?

Absolutely! You're welcome to bring your own lighting, cameras, and other equipment. Our studio is designed to accommodate various setups.

Is parking available?

Yes, we provide complimentary parking for our clients. Parking is subject to availability and is first-come, first-served.
